# gain_scale_out

`GainScaleOut` — partial scale-out at gain milestones, on ALL positions (the inverse of
`pg_profit_take`, which exempted monsters). Banks the monster giveback (S1 FY: monsters give back
~half their peak to the cloud-bottom trail — QBTS +150→+43, PAAS +96→+32) + the proved-then-died
loser peaks, while the remaining 67-75% rides the trail.

**What goes here:** the gain-milestone scale-out profit phase + its tests.
**What does not:** the fader-trim (that's `../pg_profit_take/`); full exits (that's `exit/`).
